PRODUCTS, SERVICES AND OFFERS

Here at Woodlands Away, we offer a diverse number of programs and services to suit your needs. Whether it's hiking on a mountain trail, diving in the lake or just enjoying the views, we have something for everyone at our parks and lodges. We've listed just some of the services on offer but we'll always keep adding more as the demands arise.

SPECIAL DISCOUNTS

From time to time we offer a variety of special discounts and package offers that will whet your appetite. Sign up with your e-mail address to be the first to know about any exciting discounts. There are also special limited holiday packages available.

Accomodation Only

We offer pure accomodation services only for willing visitors. The lodge will provide a visitor with food and bedding for the period of their stay. Any supplementary services such as guided tours and lake-boat rides are not included in this service.

Accomodation plus Tours

In this service package, in addition to the accomodation, visitors will also get to experience our unique and exciting tours. The lodge will provide you with a trained tour-guide who will take you to the many interesting places you may seek to visit during your stay. The boat rides on the lake are not offered as a part of this service.

Accomodation + Boat Rides+ Tours

The lodge will provide visitors additional boat rides on any lakes within the vicinity of the lodge. This service is only available at our lodges that are close to lakes. Please fill out an enquiry if you seek to know of their availability.
